FuBar_AsciiRepFu
================

Configurable ASCII reputation bar for FuBar or your minimap. [Wow 2.4.3]

Features:
  - Display faction standing as an ASCII bar in FuBar.
  - Switch watched faction from the tooltip easily.
  - Show watched factions for your alts.
